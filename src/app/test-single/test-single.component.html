MALARUSI PETCARE
Providing love and care to every paw in need.
About Us we are dedicated to improving the lives of abandoned and homeless animals. Our team of passionate animal lovers works tirelessly to provide a safe and nurturing environment for each and every pet in our care. From medical attention and behavioral rehabilitation, to finding loving forever homes, we are committed to giving all pets the second chance they deserve. We believe in creating a better world, one paw at a time.
